Introduction

Hi, if you are you looking for an experienced professional in the field of mobile communications and related technologies, then maybe I can help you.

I have been involved in many projects at the leading edge of mobile communications since 1982 and have operated as a freelance management consultant and held permanent roles. I have worked in all sectors of business including Deployment, Operations Engineering, Sales and Marketing as well as completing tasks in project and product management. I have occupied senior roles in some of the well-known mobile communications companies such as Securicor Datatrak, Orange, Hutchison Mobile Data, Dolphin and more recently UK Broadband Ltd, a division of Hong Kong based PCCW. I have also, whilst with Perot Systems, operated as a Management Consultant on a multi-million dollar business improvement project for Los Angeles Department of Water and Power.

My early mobile communications experience was gained at Dyfed Fire Brigade commissioning a previously installed brigade wide callout system utilising data carried over the Brigade PMR radio system. During my tenure with the Brigade I also and set up a communications and maintenance facility. I am also well versed in the workings of Utilities having spent considerable time, whilst with Perot Systems, understanding the business processes and communications requirements of Utilities both here and in the United States.

Since the early '90’s mobile data has been tomorrow’s success story and like tomorrow the success has never come. I have experienced the frustration and pain of mobile data and understand the issues, maybe my experience could benefit you?

During the late 90's and into 2000 I was heavily involved with the TETRA MoU as Chairman of the TETRA Business Solutions group and as Secretary of the TETRA MoU Operators' Association. You can view details of both these organisations at http://www.tetramou.com/

The pinnacle of my achievements with the MoU was to receive the 'Outstanding contribution to TETRA 2001' award at the 2001 TETRA World Congress in Nice. This was reported in the TETRA World Congress News.

For many years I had a part time consultancy arrangement with Infocell Telecoms working on Real Time Passenger Information projects in Manchester, Harrogate, Southampton, Riverside and Reading.

Since 2004 I have been employed by UK Broadband Ltd(UKB) in various project management, implementation and engineering roles implementing a TDD/CDMA network in West London and a Wimax network in Reading. UK Broadband has also rolled out WiFi And WiMax trial networks in Camden and other cities. I am currently responsible for base site engineering and have been exploring the switch from TDD-CDMA to WiMax. I have also been exploiting the minimal footprint of WiMax base sites and how to deploy them in a fast and furious fashion. We successfully transitioned our Reading TDD-CDMA network over to Wimax in May 2009. UKB has also been experimenting with using Wimax and TDD as backhaul for WiFi networks and have current trials in Reading

More recently a fair sized Ruckus mesh wifi system has been installed and is working in Reading centre.
